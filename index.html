About me

Welcome!

I am currently an undergraduate student in Computer Systems Networking at the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of Rio Grande do Norte (IFRN), Brazil. I am also a researcher in Information Security at the same institution and have a scholarship financed by the National Council for Scientific and Technological Development (CNPq). My research areas of interest are Information Security, Privacy, Malware Analysis, Software-Defined Networking, AI, and IoT.

Check more information in my bio or in my CV.
